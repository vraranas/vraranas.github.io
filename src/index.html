About Me

Software Developer

Hello, I'm Gregory Daniel Bouraoui, a Computer Science enthusiast with a strong drive for innovation and problem-solving. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a Minor in Engineering Innovation at the University of Florida, I am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology and leveraging my skills to make a meaningful impact.

Technical Proficiency

My educational journey has equipped me with a diverse technical skillset. I'm proficient in a range of programming languages, including Python, Java, C++, JavaScript, and SQL. I've honed my abilities in data analysis and visualization using popular packages like Pytest, Numpy, Matplotlib, Pandas, Scipy, and Seaborn.

Leadership and Project Management

My experience extends beyond technical expertise. As an Undergraduate Research TPM at SurfLab, I applied Agile methodology to lead cross-functional teams, ensuring the efficient development of a state-of-the-art surgical VR simulation. My leadership resulted in increased development efficiency and timely project milestones, while my ability to create and maintain UML diagrams enhanced team observability and streamlined our development process.

During my tenure as Design/IT Team Lead at Eufinity Wellness, I orchestrated strategies across multiple teams, enhancing the company's online presence and optimizing workflow for a team of 11 interns. My role involved supervising tasks, administering project management tools like Jira and Confluence, and fostering effective collaboration.
